If you are here, then you've most likely thought about how you could better improve your mental health. We're here to help! Welcome to Real Talk Counseling, where your mental health is NOT a game!

Our Mission

Make mental health awareness and services more accessible to african-american youth and young adults who are amongst the population most at risk for suicide. The stigma surrounding counseling and the socio-economic barriers to facilitating care shouldn't deter those that seek help from getting it. Our goal is to bridge the gap, and give that real talk to all that come to listen.
