What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is defined as the feelings of irritablity, jittery, and nervousness. As well as a persistent impending sense of doom/panic which can cause a high heart rate and hyperventaltion (i.e a heart attack or cardiac arrest) Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Separation Anxiety, and panic attacks can all be categorized as different types and forms of anxiety.

How does it affect people?

According to National Institute of Mental Health, Anxiety is the most common mental disorder in America. It affects roughly 18% of adults.

Forty Million adults in the United States and experience thier first anxiety episode by age 22. (facts sourced from the Anxiety and Depression Association of America)
