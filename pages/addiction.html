What is Addiction?

Addiction was previously thought of to be strictly representative of substance, and drug abuse effects on one's neurochemical makeup, but now the definition has been expanded to include any psychological dependency on internet, gambling, alchohol, sex, etc. (We all know someone a liiiiittle to occupied with thier phone.) The addiction that I am refering to for the purpose of this site is related to gaming, and the general addictive personality.

How does it affect people?

According to the National Survey on Drug Use and Health, 21.5 million Americans battled a substance use disorder in 2014.

According to the WHO (World Health Organization) the harmful use of alchohol results in the death of 3.3 million people per year.
