Our Mission




Real Talk Counseling®️ is a dedicated non-profit organization commited to reducing the accessiblity gap between culturally competent counseling service, and the people that need them. Our mission is to reduce the depression and suicide rate america by at least 3% annually. This was inspired by the exponentially increasing rate of anxiety and suicide rates during the social media era.
